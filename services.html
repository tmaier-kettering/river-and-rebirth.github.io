Services

Care is tailored to your lived experience, goals, and pace. Services are offered via telehealth across Michigan.

Perinatal Mental Health

Support for pregnancy, postpartum adjustment, and perinatal mood and anxiety disorders (PMADs). Both birthing and non-birthing parents can experience significant distress and deserve care.

EMDR Therapy

EMDR is used to process distressing memories and reduce their emotional intensity, helping your nervous system and mind move toward relief and integration.

Trauma Therapy

Trauma-focused therapy that may include IFS-informed work, attachment-based interventions, somatic-informed care, and TF-CBT techniques.

Perinatal Support for Sexual Assault Survivors

Specialized support for trauma that may be reactivated during pregnancy, birth, and postpartum transitions, using compassionate somatic and trauma-processing approaches.

Grief Therapy

Therapy for acute grief (under 6 months) and complicated grief (over 6 months), with space for your loss, identity shifts, and healing process.
