Trauma Therapy

Welcome to River & Rebirth

Compassionate telehealth counseling for clients across the state of Michigan.

I help clients move from surviving toward healing, with special focus on perinatal mental health, trauma therapy, and grief support.

Areas of Care

  • Perinatal Mental Health
  • EMDR Therapy
  • Trauma Therapy
  • Perinatal Support for Sexual Assault Survivors
  • Grief Therapy

My Approach to Care

I use a two-part model: first building safety and stability skills, then doing deeper investigative healing work so current triggers can be understood and transformed.

No matter who you are or what role you hold, you deserve to be cared for too.
