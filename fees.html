Fees & Policies

Transparent pricing to help you plan for care.

Session Fees

  • Free phone consultation (10–15 min): No charge
  • Initial intake session (60 min): $175
  • Individual therapy session (45–50 min): $150
  • Couples or family therapy session (90 min): $200

Motherhood Mosaic Package

10-session bundle (intake included), 45–50 minute sessions: $1200.

Suggested plan: 4 prenatal sessions and 6 postpartum sessions for transition support, PMAD screening/treatment, and birth or parenthood processing.

Insurance

Out-of-network and private-pay options are available. Paneling with BCBS of Michigan and HAP is in progress, but insurance billing is not currently available.

Superbills can be provided for possible reimbursement depending on your benefits.

Additional Policies

A limited number of sliding-scale spots are available for clients with financial need.

Cancellation policy: Please provide at least 24 hours notice. Late cancellations are charged 50% of the session fee; no-shows are charged the full session fee.
