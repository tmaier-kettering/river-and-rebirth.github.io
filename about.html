About River & Rebirth

I welcome clients of all ages, genders, races, religions, lifestyles, backgrounds, and family structures. I practice from a framework of inherent human dignity and provide care that supports growth and healing.

My work is rooted in evidence-based and trauma-informed care, including EMDR Therapy, IFS Therapy, attachment-based approaches, somatic-informed techniques, cognitive approaches like TF-CBT, and art therapy techniques.

Outside the office, I enjoy exploring the outdoors with my family, tackling my never-ending romantasy TBR, and crafting the day away.

Safety & Stability First

Together we build a practical toolbox for everyday resilience so you can feel more supported in the present.

Deeper Healing Work

We map the connections between current triggers and their roots, helping wounds heal so your life is not dictated by your past.
