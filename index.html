Areas of Care

How I Can Support You

Healing looks different for everyone. Whether you are navigating the emotional landscape of new parenthood, working through the effects of trauma, or looking for an evidence-based approach to shift stuck patterns — there is a path forward.

Perinatal Mental Health

Support for anxiety, depression, grief, birth trauma, and the profound identity shifts that accompany pregnancy, postpartum, and infant loss.

Learn more

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing — an evidence-based, body-informed approach that helps your nervous system process and release distressing memories.

Learn more

Trauma Therapy

Trauma therapy utilizing IFS Therapy, attachment-based therapy, somatic experiencing practices, and TF-CBT techniques to help process painful experiences and restore a stronger sense of safety and connection.

Learn more

Perinatal Support for Sexual Assault Survivors

Specialized perinatal support that honors trauma held in the body and addresses what may be reactivated during pregnancy and birth, using somatic experiencing practices alongside EMDR and trauma-focused care.

Get support

Grief Therapy

Support for both acute grief (less than 6 months from a loss) and complicated grief (more than 6 months), with care tailored to your unique relationship, story, and healing pace.

Get support

About Your Therapist

You Deserve Care That Truly Sees You

My work is rooted in curiosity, warmth, and deep respect for the resilience you already carry. I use evidence-based approaches including EMDR and somatic-informed techniques, woven together in a way that honors your unique history and goals.

I have a special passion for supporting individuals and families during the perinatal period — from fertility challenges and pregnancy loss through postpartum adjustment and beyond. This season of life can be profoundly hard, and no one should have to navigate it alone.

  • Trauma-informed & attachment-focused
  • Non-judgmental, culturally sensitive approach
  • Collaborative — you are the expert on your own life
